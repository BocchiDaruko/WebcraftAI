## 💡 Tips for better results

1. **Be specific** — Mention the style, colors, or technology you prefer
2. **Include context** — "for a fitness app" vs "for a blog page"
3. **Ask for iterations** — "Now make it more minimalist" or "Add a dark version"
4. **Request explanations** — "Explain why you chose this typography"
5. **Combine requests** — "Generate the component AND explain how to customize it"
